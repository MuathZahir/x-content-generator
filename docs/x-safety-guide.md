# X Safety Guide

penn AI is designed to keep the user in control. It suggests replies, but it does not post, like, repost, follow, DM, scrape feeds, or run campaigns.

This does not guarantee account safety. X says users are responsible for actions taken by their accounts and that spammy or duplicative mentions/replies can lead to enforcement. Treat this guide as risk reduction, not immunity.

Sources:

- X automation rules: https://help.x.com/articles/20174732
- X automation rules, updated April 2026: https://help.x.com/en/rules-and-policies/twitter-automation
- X authenticity/platform manipulation policy: https://help.x.com/ro/rules-and-policies/platform-manipulation
- X account behavior best practices: https://help.x.com/articles/110878

## Safe Operating Rules

- Keep all posting manual.
- Edit every generated reply before posting.
- Reply at normal human speed.
- Do not bulk reply to many accounts.
- Do not post duplicative replies.
- Do not use keyword searches to mass-target strangers.
- Do not force product mentions.
- Do not repeatedly include links.
- Avoid trending hashtags for promotion.
- Avoid replying with sensitive, profane, or adult content unless the conversation clearly warrants it.
- Stop if replies are hidden, rate-limited, flagged, or receiving spam-like feedback.

## Recommended Workflow

1. Open an X post or reply composer.
2. Expand **Context sent** and confirm the visible context is appropriate.
3. Generate suggestions.
4. Pick one only if it adds something specific.
5. Edit the reply into your own wording.
6. Remove product mentions unless they are directly relevant.
7. Post manually.

## Product Mention Rules

Mention a product only when:

- The thread is directly about the problem your product solves.
- You can frame it as a personal example, not a pitch.
- The reply is useful even if the product sentence is removed.

Do not mention a product when:

- The topic is only vaguely adjacent.
- You are replying mainly to get visibility.
- You would need a link for the reply to make sense.
- You already mentioned the same product in several recent replies.

## Suggested Daily Limits for Early Testing

These are conservative product guidelines, not official X limits.

- Start with 5-10 assisted replies per day.
- Avoid bursts. Space replies out naturally.
- Keep most replies link-free.
- Keep most replies product-free.
- Increase only after you see normal engagement and no filtering.

## Warning Signs

Pause usage if:

- Replies stop receiving normal visibility.
- Replies appear under spam/low-quality sections.
- X prompts you with unusual verification or rate-limit messages.
- People complain that your replies look automated.
- You notice repeated wording across replies.

## Implementation Boundaries

penn AI should continue to avoid:

- Auto-posting.
- Bulk reply queues.
- Lead scraping.
- Automated mentions.
- Automated DMs.
- Automated follows or unfollows.
- Automated reposts or likes.
